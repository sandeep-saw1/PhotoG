Welcome to the roadmap for BTech CSE students aiming for a successful career in the tech industry!

Roadmap

Year 1: Foundations

Focus on basics: Programming, Data Structures, and Algorithms.

Year 2: Specialization

Choose elective subjects and engage in projects and internships.

Year 3: Advanced Skills

Focus on advanced topics and participate in coding competitions.

Year 4: Job Preparation

Resume building and interview preparation.
